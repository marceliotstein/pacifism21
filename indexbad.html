But ... one crucial piece is missing.

Where is Pacifism in 2015?

  • Why is the word rarely used?
  • Why do so few people believe that a popular movement against war can succeed?
  • Why do the enormous practial benefits of a more peaceful planet get so little attention?

Rescue the word, rescue the idea.

  • By opening a vibrant public conversation about the meaning of the word "pacifism" we can call attention to the important ideas the word represents.

Peace is the Essential Ingredient.

War is toxic to progress, on every critical issue:
  • Individual Privacy
  • Environment
  • Economy
  • Democracy and Free Press
  • Human Rights
  • Prevention of Slavery and Genocide

Introducing ... Pacifism21.org

  • Mission statement: "Some believe that pacifism was killed by the 20th Century. In fact it will save the 21st."
