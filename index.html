But ... one crucial piece is missing.

Where is Pacifism in 2015?

  • Why is the word so rarely used?
  • Why do so few people believe that a popular movement to reduce global militarism can succeed?
  • Why do the enormous practical benefits of a more peaceful planet get so little attention?

Rescue the word, rescue the idea.

  • By opening a vibrant public conversation about the meaning of the word "pacifism" we can call attention to the important ideas the word represents.

Peace is the Essential Ingredient.

  • Individual Privacy
  • Environment
  • Economy
  • Democracy and Free Press
  • Human Rights
  • Prevention of Slavery and Genocide

Introducing ... Pacifism21.org

  • A website and advocacy organization (501c3 non-profit).
  • Mission statement: "Some believe that pacifism was killed by the 20th Century. We will prove that it is essential for the 21st."

What Will Pacifism21.org Do?

Social Publishing

  • Blogs: breaking news, topic specialists
  • Deep integration with Facebook, Twitter, Instagram
  • Fun, lively, visual content (daily comic strip, lists, infographics)

Community and Connection

  • Forums and discussions
  • Polls and quizzes
  • Member profiles
  • Activist onboarding and mentoring
  • Essay contests, workshops, school outreach

Journalism and Book Publishing

  • Investigations, research and data analysis
  • Book reviews
  • Interviews with authors, activists and relevant celebrities
  • Philosophy and history of pacifism
  • Original books and e-books

Crowdfunding and Grants

  • Project-based social fundraising on Indiegogo
  • Grant opportunities (USIP, NEA, NEH, Carnegie, Friends)
  • Membership organization with small annual fees

Conference and Meetups

  • Urban and regional meetups
  • Scheduled mentoring sessions for activists
  • Annual major conference!
